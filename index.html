HANNIBAL

Hannibal is an American psychological horror-thriller where it focuses on the relationship between FBI special investigator Will Graham and Dr. Hannibal Lecter, a forensic psychiatrist that is secretly a cannibalistic serial killer. The series delves into the psychological and emotional dynamics between the two characters as they become intertwined in a cat-and-mouse game of manipulation and moral ambiguity.

Main Cast :
  • Hugh Dancy as Will Graham
  • Mads Mikkelsen as Dr. Hannibal Lecter
  • Caroline Dhavernas as Dr. Alana Bloom
  • Laurence Fishburne as Jack Crawford
